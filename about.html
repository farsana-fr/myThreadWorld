About

l found my hobby during the pandemic while l was working from home. My Big Thanks to my li'l sister for introducing me to the world of embroidery.After my first work, l fell in love with this art form. l started gifting the handmade things to my family and relatives. l got a great support and encouragement from my Family, especially my Mother with whom l share all the work progress.

After few months l found yet another intersting art form called Crochet. l was a bit nervous to buy all that stuff before trying out a single crochet stitch in my life. Finally one day the idea of crocheting hit me and with encouragement from my Mom l bought a small hook to learn.After couple of days, learned the very basic single crochet stitch from YT tutorials. Now, finally l accured the courage to buy a new set of crochet hooks and yarn from MagicNeedles.

Now, l want to extend my research in threads to Macrame works, made a keychain and plant hanger. Looking forward to explore more on Macrame.

This is all about the my tiny ThreadWorld. Hope you liked it.
